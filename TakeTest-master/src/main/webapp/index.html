Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does automated grading work?

    Automated grading applies to objective questions like multiple-choice, true/false, and matching questions. The system instantly evaluates responses and provides scores, allowing for faster feedback and results.

  • Can users retake assessments?

    Yes, you can configure settings to allow retakes based on your assessment policies. You can also set time intervals or conditions before a user can attempt the test again.

  • Is there a limit to the number of participants

    No, our platform is scalable and can accommodate an unlimited number of users, making it suitable for both small classes and large organizations.

  • How do I get started?

    Simply sign up for an account, and our onboarding guide will walk you through setting up your first assessment. You can also schedule a demo with our support team for a more personalized walkthrough.

  • How does the assessment portal prevent cheating during exams?

    The assessment portal uses a combination of advanced technologies to prevent cheating. This includes features like real-time monitoring of user behavior. Suspicious activities such as tab switching, copy-pasting, or unusual patterns are detected and flagged for review.
